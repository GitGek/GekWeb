Episode IV: THE COMPUTEBLADE AWAKENS

It is a time of innovation. Rebel engineers, striking from their hidden labs, have won their first victory against the boring status quo.

During the battle, Master Ivan, fearless founder of Uptime Industries and father of ComputeBlade, unveiled the Lemony Enclosure — a device of such elegance, it left even the Empire’s thermal engineers speechless.

Leading the PCB rebellion, Ivan inspired a generation with projects that defied convention, broke expectations, and pulsed with creative energy.

Today we celebrate the birth of a legend. The Force, the Firmware, and the Frontend salute him.

May your systems be stable, your packets swift, and your circuits forever unburned.

Happy Birthday, Ivan — and may the source be with you!
